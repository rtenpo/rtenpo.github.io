NETWORK AND TELECOM (NT)

The objective of this branch is to meet the need of the socio-economic sector in terms of human resources capable of making an easy approach to any concern for the management of business networks and telecommunications. This training offers lessons focused on practice compatible with professional activity. The NT specialty reviews a state of the art of Networks and Telecommunications tools and methods and leads to real expertise in the management of cross-functional network projects.
